Privacy Policy

All in 1 Ad auto-skipper — Last updated: June 30, 2026

This privacy policy describes how the "All in 1 Ad auto-skipper" browser extension (the "Extension") handles user data.

Data we collect

The Extension does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any personal or sensitive user data. We do not use analytics, tracking, advertising identifiers, or any third-party data services. No information about you or your browsing activity ever leaves your device.

How the Extension works

The Extension runs entirely on your own device. It detects video advertisements on supported streaming platforms and triggers the skip action through the page's own player interface. All processing happens locally in your browser.

Permissions

The Extension requests the minimum permissions required to provide its single purpose:

  • Host permissions (limited to supported streaming sites) — to run the skip logic on the player pages where advertisements appear.
  • Tabs — to determine which supported streaming site is active in the current tab so the correct skip logic is applied. No browsing history or tab content is read, stored, or transmitted.
  • Storage — to save your local preferences (such as which platforms are enabled and the on/off state) on your own device. These settings are never sent to any external server.

Third parties

The Extension does not share data with any third party, because it does not collect any data in the first place.

Limited Use compliance

The use of information received through Google APIs and the Chrome platform will adhere to the Chrome Web Store User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes in the future, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
